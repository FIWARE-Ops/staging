CuraVesta© is an innovative assistance system developed by ENGINEERING to support home care, specifically designed for individuals who care for and nurse their relatives at home. This mHealth solution is aimed at providing effective and lasting support to caregivers, enabling their loved ones to live independently and safely in their own homes. The system is accessible via mobile devices such as smartphones, making it convenient for caregivers to use anytime and anywhere.

The system comprises three main components that facilitate home care:

  • Nursing Guide: This component is designed to ease the accomplishment of daily caregiving tasks and alleviate the mental stress associated with nurturing. It provides caregivers with knowledge on how to professionally execute typical tasks and informs them about available resources, directly at the point of care.
  • Exchange Platform: This feature connects caregivers with other relatives and essential service providers such as doctors, nursing services, and telemedicine providers. It fosters a network of support and information exchange, enhancing the caregiving experience.
  • Purchasing Avatar: This tool assists caregivers in purchasing necessary items such as medicine, resources, and meals. It monitors stock levels and prompts caregivers to reorder supplies in a timely manner, especially before public holidays, ensuring that they are always well-prepared.

Within these components, CuraVesta© offers a variety of functions to support both the caregiver and the person in need of care. The main functions include:

  1. Daily Routine Module: This module assists in documenting and controlling the nursing progress, helping caregivers manage daily tasks efficiently.
  2. Electronic Patient File: This feature allows for the creation of an electronic patient file that can be exported and linked to family members and general practitioners, ensuring seamless communication and information sharing.
  3. Accelerated Processes: The system streamlines application and order processes, as well as time management with cost objects and care providers, independent of suppliers.
  4. Integration of Digital Medical Products: CuraVesta© supports the integration of digital medical products and Ambient Assisted Living (AAL) technologies, enhancing the quality of care provided.
  5. Multimedia Transfer of Nursing Know-How: The system facilitates the transfer of nursing knowledge through multimedia, providing caregivers with valuable insights and training.
  6. Video Communication Support: Caregivers can receive advice from experienced nursing personnel and doctors via video communication, using the patient file as a reference, through a dedicated support center.

The CuraVesta© system is initially provided in a restricted basic version. If user acceptance is demonstrated, it will be further developed in at least three stages, ensuring continuous improvement and adaptation to the needs of caregivers and their loved ones. This comprehensive approach makes CuraVesta© a valuable tool for enhancing the home care experience, providing caregivers with the resources and support they need to deliver high-quality care.
