Plug&Sense is a versatile IoT device designed to cater to a wide range of applications by offering several user-friendly features and benefits. It is particularly useful in sectors such as Smart Cities, Smart Agriculture, Industry 4.0, and Asset Tracking, among others. Here are some of the key use cases and features of Plug&Sense:

  • Easy Sensor Integration: The device allows for the easy attachment of sensor probes by simply screwing them into the bottom sockets. This feature enables users to quickly add new sensing capabilities to existing networks, facilitating rapid deployment and scalability. Additionally, sensor probes can be easily replaced, minimizing maintenance costs and ensuring the longevity of the sensor network.

  • Solar-Powered Battery Recharge: Plug&Sense offers both internal and external solar panel options for battery recharging, in addition to standard USB recharging. The external solar panel is mounted on a 45ยบ holder to maximize performance in outdoor installations, while the internal solar panel is embedded on the front of the enclosure, ideal for space-constrained environments. The rechargeable battery, with a capacity of 6600 mAh, ensures continuous operation even during periods of low sunlight.

  • Outdoor Installation: The device is equipped with holders specifically designed for outdoor installations on streetlights and building fronts. Metal cable ties are provided for easy attachment to streetlight posts, allowing for quick and secure installation. Once installed, the device automatically begins transmitting sensor data to the Gateway.

  • Flexible Communication Options: Plug&Sense supports multiple radio options for communication with the Gateway, including 802.15.4, 868, and 900 MHz for local networks, WiFi for connecting to any WiFi access point, and 4G for direct cloud communication without an intermediate Gateway. It also supports Sigfox and LoRaWAN for long-range IoT applications.

  • Real-Time Tracking: The 4G module includes a GPS receiver, enabling real-time tracking applications. This feature is particularly beneficial for asset tracking and monitoring mobile units.

  • Industrial Protocol Compatibility: Plug&Sense can connect with common industrial communication protocols such as RS-232, RS-485, Modbus, 4-20 mA (current loop), and CAN Bus. This compatibility opens up a wide range of applications in industrial sensing, automation, military, automotive, vehicle tracking, and remote factory management.

  • External SIM Socket: Models equipped with 4G radio feature an external SIM socket, allowing developers to easily add or change carrier SIM cards. This flexibility enables connection to any 4G/3G/GPRS/GSM cellular network, enhancing the device's adaptability to different network environments.

Overall, Plug&Sense is a highly adaptable IoT solution that provides robust features for various applications, ensuring ease of use, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness in deploying and maintaining sensor networks.
