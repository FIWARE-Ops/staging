Dragsa Port is an IoT platform designed to connect and digitize port infrastructure and operations, enabling real-time data processing and contextual intelligence through FIWARE integration. It transforms sensor and equipment data into actionable insights to optimize maritime logistics, asset utilization, and service management. The platform incorporates remote control, AI-driven decision support, and predictive maintenance tools for smarter port management.

  • Comprehensive monitoring and diagnostics of port assets in real time
  • Advanced analytics to anticipate and resolve operational incidents
  • Automation of critical processes for improved efficiency and safety
  • Remote technical support to minimize downtime and ensure continuous port operations

The main challenge is to demonstrate the capability of our technology to integrate seamlessly with the FIWARE Context Broker by using data models fully aligned with FIWARE standards and implementing NGSI-based CRUD operations. These operations enable the transmission, reception, and real-time processing of contextual information between IoT devices and the platform.

This integration will support real-time monitoring of a smart seaport, providing visibility into port operations, environmental conditions, and logistics, as well as a parking and charging solution for personal mobility vehicles.
