FIWARE Global Summit: AI and Natural Language for Smart Cities: Turning Data into Action

Tech & Trends -

As urban areas face increasing risks from climate change, infrastructure strain, and public safety challenges, decision-makers need tools that not only process vast amounts of data—but also make it accessible and actionable. This session explores how cutting-edge technologies like satellite-based Earth Observation, Generative AI, and Large Language Models (LLMs) are converging to empower smarter, more resilient cities.
