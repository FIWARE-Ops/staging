FIWARE Global Summit: National & International Aid and State Grants

Circular Economy & Urban Development -

This session highlights how Moroccan cities can leverage global support from organizations such as GIZ, UNDP, alongside local entities such as EFE Maroc. It will focus on available funding and grants, including those from the Fonds de Développement de la Compétitivité and Fondation Hassan II, to accelerate urban development and smart city initiatives. Participants will learn how to access these resources to enhance infrastructure and sustainability in preparation for the FIFA World Cup 2030.
