FIWARE Global Summit: Governing Trust: Legal and Data Space Frameworks for Secure Cities and Resilient Event Management

Research & Development -

This session explores how legal frameworks and data space governance models can ensure trust, security, and resilience in smart cities and large-scale event management. With a focus on policy, research, and academic perspectives, it will examine how legal clarity, data sovereignty, and interoperable data sharing contribute to secure, citizen-centric digital ecosystems.
