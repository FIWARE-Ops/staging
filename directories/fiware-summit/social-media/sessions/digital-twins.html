FIWARE Global Summit: Digital Twins

Tech & Trends -

This session will present use cases showing why NGSI-LD is the right standard for implementing Digital Twins using FIWARE, highlighting the new graph retrieval feature. Participants will have an outlook on the evolution of NGSI-LD, including the handling of service execution. Based on a smart water use case, participants will see how service execution enables the integration of analytics for predicting, exploring possible future developments under different conditions and executing complex actuation requests.
