FIWARE Global Summit: Building Bridges Through Science: International Academic Cooperation for Digital Transformation

Research & Development -

This session brings together leading professors and researchers from Morocco and Europe to explore avenues for sustained academic collaboration in the field of digital innovation. Hosted during the FIWARE Global Summit, the discussion aims to lay the groundwork for long-term cooperation between the FIWARE Foundation and leading Academic Institutions in Morocco. The discussion aims at opening the door to high-impact joint participation in EU-funded projects, as well as national and multilateral calls supported by Moroccan ministries and international agencies. Together, we will explore how open source, standards-based technologies and academic partnerships can catalyze real-world impact and accelerate sustainable digitalization across borders.
