FIWARE Global Summit: When Intelligence Meets Endurance: AI & Data-Driven Cities for the FIFA World Cup 2030

Sustainable Smart City -

This session explores how AI and data technologies can elevate urban management in preparation for the FIFA World Cup 2030. From predictive planning to real-time simulations, smart tools enable cities to optimize security, mobility, tourism, and resource management. By fostering data interoperability and cross-sector collaboration, Morocco can ensure agile, informed decision-making. These innovations are key to creating resilient, efficient, and welcoming host cities for the world stage.
