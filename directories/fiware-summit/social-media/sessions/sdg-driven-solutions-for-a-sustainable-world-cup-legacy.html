FIWARE Global Summit: SDG-Driven Solutions for a Sustainable World Cup Legacy

Sustainable Smart City -

This session highlights how sustainable energy and water management are essential to building climate-resilient cities ready to host the FIFA World Cup 2030. By integrating green technologies, digital water solutions, and circular economy models, Morocco can ensure resource efficiency and environmental stewardship. These innovations not only support the demands of a global event but also lay the groundwork for a lasting, sustainable urban legacy.
