FIWARE Global Summit: Next-Generation Governance: Where Smart Meets Digital

Smart Governance -

This session highlights how Morocco’s digital transformation of public services is a strategic enabler for hosting the FIFA World Cup 2030. By modernizing administration, enhancing transparency through data sharing, and leveraging AI for smarter public services, the country is building agile and citizen-focused governance. These advancements ensure efficiency, trust, and readiness for large-scale global events. The conference will explore how such innovations contribute to a seamless, secure, and inclusive World Cup experience.
