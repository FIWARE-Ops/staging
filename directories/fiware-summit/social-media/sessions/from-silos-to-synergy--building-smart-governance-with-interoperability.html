FIWARE Global Summit: From Silos to Synergy: Building Smart Governance with Interoperability

Data Spaces & Interoperability -

This session explores how interoperability, open standards, and data governance are transforming smart governance from fragmented initiatives into cohesive, future-ready strategies. By examining innovative approaches—from digital transformation frameworks and open-source data platforms to standardized building information models and AI-driven housing solutions—this session highlights how breaking silos enables more efficient, inclusive, and sustainable urban development.
