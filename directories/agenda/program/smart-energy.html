FIWARE Global Summit: Smart Energy

AI & Data-Driven Urban Management -

In recent years, the European Commission and EU countries have set up numerous large-scale pilot projects to contribute to the digitization of the energy industry across Europe and beyond. The most important goals of this endeavor are reaching a common European energy data space to facilitate infrastructure, planning and monitoring, while enabling the deployment of trusted, interoperable, resilient and secure data sharing to accelerate the deployment of AI-based data-driven applications to speed up green energy transition. In this panel we will bring together esteemed pitches from international experts to present concrete pilots and use cases and discuss on how and what extent FIWARE solutions and underlying open architecture

  • seamless integrate extended IoT sensing capabilities with the digitized energy infrastructure with a view to increase its scalability and its adaptation potential against a variety of different energy regulated and non-regulated markets across different countries;
  • Contribute to enable secure and trusted Data Sharing Framework within the Common European Energy Data Space (CEEDS), while providing an appropriate framework to effectively address cyber-security challenges to the energy grids
  • Facilitate the development of AI-powered data-driven Digital Twins for predicting, optimizing and monitoring energy digitized critical infrastructure by leveraging NGSI-LD and Smart Data Models The session, moderated by Massimo Bertoncini, includes four pitches from international experts, followed by a panel discussion and an interactive Q&A session with the audience.
