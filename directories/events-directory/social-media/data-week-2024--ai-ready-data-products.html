FIWARE Conference: Data Week 2024: AI-ready Data Products

On 10 December 2024, FIWARE's CEO, Chandra Challagonda, will join a distinguished panel at Data Week 2024 in Luxembourg to discuss the transformative role of AI-ready Data Products. This session, scheduled from 11:30 to 13:00 CET at Novotel Luxembourg Kirchberg, will explore how these data products revolutionise data sharing, AI workflows, and business models. Key topics include defining and optimizing AI-ready data products, addressing critical aspects like data provenance, quality, and compliance, and exploring the influence of the AI Act on shaping data frameworks.

The panel will feature industry and academic leaders from BDVA, Eindhoven University of Technology, Dawex, and the European Commission, offering a rich diversity of insights. This event promises to be a crucial platform for understanding how AI-ready data products can empower innovative business models and drive the future of AI applications, making it a must-attend for professionals passionate about AI and data intersections.
