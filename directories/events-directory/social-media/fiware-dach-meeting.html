FIWARE DACH Meeting

Join us for a networking event hosted by the City of Herne and FIWARE Foundation on February 4, 2025, from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM at Mulvany Villa, Shamrock Park, Herne. This event kicks off Herne’s Smart City Days, taking place on February 5–6, 2025, under the theme “Transformation Process of the Ruhr Metropolis.”

Attendees can look forward to insights from our new FIWARE CEO, Chandra Challagonda, as well as discussions about FIWARE's strategic vision for the future. Topics will include AI, Data Spaces, AR/VR applications for digital twins, and much more. This event also provides valuable opportunities to connect with leaders from various cities, businesses, and organizations.

Don't miss this chance to engage with decision-makers from the business, political, and urban sectors across the DACH region!
