Smart Metropolis FIWARE iHub Summit 2025

On July 21 and 22, Natal will host the Smart Metropolis FIWARE iHub Summit 2025, an event that brings together public managers, experts, and entrepreneurs to shape the future of smart cities in Brazil. The summit will take place at the Instituto Metrópole Digital (IMD/UFRN) and will mark the official launch of the Smart Metropolis FIWARE iHub, which is the first officially recognized FIWARE innovation center in the country.

This initiative is the culmination of over a decade of IMD's efforts in digital transformation for public management, with a strong focus on open and interoperable technologies. Designed specifically for mayors and municipal leaders, the event promotes a hands-on innovation agenda that connects real-world experiences with technology-driven solutions for smarter governance.

The event is organized by Smart Metropolis FIWARE iHub, iConects, the Consórcio CIGA, and UFRN, and sponsored by Grupo Interjato.
