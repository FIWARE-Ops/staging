UN Open Source Week 2025

The UN Open Source Week 2025 will take place from June 16-20 in New York City. The event aims to foster global dialogue and collaboration on open source technology, digital public infrastructure (DPI), and the development of innovative solutions to tackle global challenges. Since its inception in 2023, the event has garnered significant international interest, highlighting a strong demand for a UN-led forum dedicated to open source and digital cooperation.

FIWARE Foundation will be organizing an official 80-minute side event with the theme of Digital Public Infrastructures on June 20. The event provides a unique opportunity to showcase the impact of open source and interoperable digital infrastructure—core to FIWARE’s mission—in driving global innovation, sustainable development, and digital public goods.
