FIWARE Webinar: Intro to FGS25

We are honored to announce that Dr. Aawatif Hayar, Chairman of the Smart Cities Morocco Alliance and former Moroccan Minister of Solidarity, Social Integration, and Family, will join our upcoming FIWARE Global Summit 2025 Info Session on Tuesday, April 1st, from 14:30 to 15:30 (CEST). Dr. Hayar will provide insights into the Smart Cities Morocco Roadshow and the Smart Cities Morocco Alliance.

The session will also include an introduction to the FIWARE Global Summit by FIWARE Foundation CEO Chandra Challagonda, and will address our redesigned FIWARE Training, ticketing, sponsorship opportunities, and tips for planning your visit. Join us to learn more about this unique opportunity to witness Morocco's commitment to building a smarter and more sustainable future and how you, too, can tap into this growing market.
