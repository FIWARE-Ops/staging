Expo I Smart Cities

FIWARE COO Alberto Abella will speak on a stage of Expo Smart Cities Colombia about the best ways using FIWARE technology and Community solutions to accelerate and reduce the cost of smart services for citizens, Expo I is a platform and summit that calls governments, institutions, companies and professionals to the innovative and sustainable development of cities and territories. In this great event focused on the sectors of cities, territories and smart infrastructures you can find the main actors and leading companies of the smartcities industries presenting solutions and technologies oriented to the future in an innovative exhibition and commercial exhibition, as well as a first-level academic agenda where national mayors, international mayors, national government, invited foreign governments, entities and technology experts participate.
