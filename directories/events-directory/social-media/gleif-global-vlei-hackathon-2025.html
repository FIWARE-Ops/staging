GLEIF Global vLEI Hackathon 2025

The Global Legal Entity Identifier Foundation (GLEIF) has officially launched the vLEI Hackathon 2025, a global innovation challenge calling on developers, entrepreneurs, and digital trust stakeholders to reimagine how digital trust is built using the verifiable Legal Entity Identifier (vLEI) in the following key strategic sectors:

  • Digital Assets & Financial Infrastructure

  • Industry 4.0: Manufacturing, Commerce & Finance

  • Trade, Supply Chain & SME Finance

This fully remote hackathon offers winning teams a unique opportunity to showcase their solutions at in-person forums in New York, Frankfurt, and Hong Kong.

This is your chance to:

  • Build solutions that combine vLEI (Verifiable Legal Entity Identifier) with FIWARE open-source technologies.

  • Tackle real-world problem statements in Digital Assets, Supply Chain, Finance, Industry 4.0, IoT, and ESG reporting.

  • Gain global visibility, mentorship, and the opportunity to pitch your ideas at international events.

  • Compete for prizes while shaping the standards that will define trust in the digital economy.

  • Collaborate with international leaders and innovators across finance, trade, supply chain, and Industry 4.0.

  • Showcase solutions combining FIWARE’s data space and smart services technologies with GLEIF’s vLEI standard.

  • Gain visibility, access to global networks, and opportunities to scale solutions into real-world markets.

  • Contribute to shaping the next generation of digital trust standards.

Key Dates:

  • July 14 - August 29: Registration & proposal submissions

  • September 7: Shortlisting complete

  • October 12: Final solution submissions due (Themes 1 & 3)

  • November 16: Final solution submissions due (Theme 2)

  • November - December: Winning teams present their solutions in one of the three global forums
