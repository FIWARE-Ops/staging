FIWARE Training

The two-day training at the 2nd International Conference on Digital Sovereignty 2025 offers a complete journey through FIWARE’s role in enabling trusted, interoperable data spaces.

Day I (Monday 27) delivers hands-on technical training with FIWARE Technical Evangelist Jason Fox, covering NGSI-LD, smart data models, IoT integration, machine learning, and MLOps.

The Training will cover the most discussed topics on:

  • Background and Core Concepts of Data in FIWARE: JSON-LD and Linked Data, Smart Data Models, NGSI-LD
  • NGSI-LD and Distributed Operations: Solving the Challenges of Distributed Operations within Data Spaces, Temporal, Language Maps, Raw JSON, Multi-Relationship
  • Practical Applications: Machine Learning, MLOps and FIWARE, Connecting to IoT

Day II (Tuesday 28) bridges technology and strategy: the morning explores securing distributed architectures, implementing sovereign data spaces, and monetising services with AI while the afternoon business session examines how FIWARE empowers digital sovereignty, fosters cross-domain innovation, and unlocks new market opportunities in the data economy.

During the Tech Session, Jason Fox, FIWARE Technical Evangelist, will deep dive into the following topics:

  • Understanding Distributed Security and Data Spaces: How to secure architectures, Mechanisms of Distributed Trust, FIWARE Data Space Connector
  • Monetising Services: Use of FIWARE with LLMs
  • LLM Practical session

Digital Sovereignty with FIWARE: Business Session by Chandra Challagonda, CEO at FIWARE Foundation

In the Data Economy, digital sovereignty is a strategic business necessity. Data spaces enable trusted ecosystems where data and AI-driven services can be shared under clear governance for interoperability and compliance.

FIWARE provides the technology stack for data spaces, combining the NGSI-LD standard, smart data models, Data Space Connector, and Marketplace technologies, developed under the umbrella of collaboration activities with Gaia-X as well as other open-source initiatives such as Prometheus-X.

Its open-source, standards-based approach ensures sovereignty, interoperability, and trust, avoiding vendor lock-in and enabling cross-domain collaboration. This way, FIWARE empowers public and private stakeholders to unlock new markets, accelerate innovation, and strengthen competitiveness in a data-driven world.

Business Session Key Topics - Part I

  1. Digital sovereignty as a strategic imperative – How to turn sovereignty policies into competitive advantage, with practical examples from real-world implementations.
  2. From data spaces to value ecosystems – Moving beyond simple data sharing to enable AI, big data analytics, real-time IoT services, and sector-specific applications.
  3. Trust, governance, and security frameworks – Ensuring interoperability, compliance, and multi-stakeholder collaboration in sovereign AI and data ecosystems.
  4. FIWARE technology stack in action – Core concepts (NGSI-LD, JSON-LD, Smart Data Models), IoT integration, data persistence, and the FIWARE Data Space

Business Session Key Topics - Part II

  1. AI enablement through FIWARE – Applying machine learning and MLOps within sovereign data architectures to accelerate innovation.
  2. Collaborative ecosystem engagement – Partnerships and alignment with Gaia-X and Prometheus-X to strengthen European and global interoperability.
  3. Open source as a sovereignty enabler – Avoiding vendor lock-in and ensuring scalable, cross-domain, cross-border operations with full transparency.
  4. Business impact of digital sovereignty – Unlocking new markets, driving innovation, and building long-term competitiveness through open, trusted, and sovereign digital infrastructures.
