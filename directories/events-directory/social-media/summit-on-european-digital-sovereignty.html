Summit on European Digital Sovereignty

In an era of rapid technological change and shifting global dynamics, European digital sovereignty has become more critical than ever. To strengthen Europe’s capacity for independent, secure, and innovation-driven digital growth, the governments of Germany and France, together with other EU Member States, are convening the Summit on European Digital Sovereignty.

This high-level event will bring together political leaders, industry representatives, academia, and civil society to exchange insights and define collective strategies for Europe’s digital future.

The discussions will explore European responses to global digital challenges, with a focus on:

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Startups and innovation ecosystems
  • Cloud and data sovereignty

Representatives from FIWARE Foundation, including FIWARE Foundation CEO Chandra Challagonda, will be present at the event as part of the Community-X project. The project marks a groundbreaking milestone for Etteln, Germany, propelling the village to the forefront of global digital innovation. Building on its FIWARE-based digital platform, Etteln is now transitioning to a Gaia-X compliant data space, paving the way for Europe’s first municipal data space.

Initially focusing on municipal services, energy, and mobility, this shared data ecosystem will drive smarter, more efficient, and sustainable local governance. Recognized as Germany’s most digital village and winner of the 2024 IEEE Smart City Award, Etteln continues to set new standards in community-driven digital transformation. Through Community-X, the town’s platform will evolve into a DSBA/DSSC Blueprint 2.0, offering a scalable model for other communities in Germany and across Europe to follow.
