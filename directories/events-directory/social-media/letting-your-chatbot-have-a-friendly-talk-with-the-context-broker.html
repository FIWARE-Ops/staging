Letting Your Chatbot Have a Friendly Talk With the Context Broker

Unlock the potential of your FIWARE Smart App by harnessing the power of natural language. Learn a strategy to seamlessly integrate LLM-driven capabilities into your existing FIWARE deployment, opening the door to agentic interaction over your contextual data, and unlock new opportunities for smarter, context-aware applications.
