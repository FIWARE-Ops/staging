FIWARE Conference: Smart City Expo World Congress

Held in Barcelona since 2011, Smart City Expo World Congress (in short: SCEWC) is the international leading event for cities. Their mission is to empower cities and collectivise urban innovation across the globe. Through promoting social innovation and identifying collaboration opportunities, the event is dedicated to creating a better future for cities and their citizens.

FIWARE is a regular participant of this event with own booth to accommodate FIWARE Community members, projects and partners, strong agenda to present the latest references and impact stories. As a regular participant to this trade show and conference, we successfully accomodate every year many of our partners with us to make the FIWARE booth even more compelling. We are planning not only to give our community workspace to be present on the event, but also run demonstrations of the most successful references, have the opportunity to meet with clients or future partners on our meeting tables and to be promoted through our SoMe channels.
