Cities Climate Action Summit 2025

The Cities Climate Action Summit is a key event for city officials and local governments to explore how urban areas can drive climate change solutions. It brings together city leaders, policymakers, green finance experts, and technology innovators to tackle urban climate challenges effectively.

We are proud to partner with Smart Cities World for the Cities Climate Action Summit 2025. FIWARE and the FIWARE Community recognize that climate change is affecting societies and daily life. Our community already proudly presents a variety of open source applications and solutions that positively impact climate change and urban sustainability.
