FIWARE Summit: FIWARE Global Summit 2025

We are excited to invite you to the upcoming FIWARE Global Summit in Rabat, Morocco, from May 22nd to 23rd, 2025.

For the first time ever, the FIWARE Global Summit will be held in Morocco, highlighting the innovative potential of smart and sustainable territories beyond Europe. As Morocco prepares for the 2030 FIFA World Cup, the country is making significant investments in cutting-edge infrastructure, advanced mobility solutions, and transformative digital initiatives.

Hosted by Rabat Smart City, the FIWARE Global Summit 2025 offers a unique opportunity to witness Morocco's commitment to shaping a smarter, more sustainable future. As part of the Smart Cities Morocco Roadshow and with the support of UM6P and the Smart Moroccan Alliance for Resilient Territories (SMART), the summit aims to promote digital innovation.

Why Join the Event?

  1. Engage with High-Level Decision-Makers: The event connects Moroccan ministries, regional governors, major ICT companies, and startups in search of innovative digital solutions.
  2. Tap into a Growing Market: Morocco serves as a strategic gateway between Europe, Africa, and the Middle East, presenting opportunities in smart cities, AI, smart governance, cybersecurity, and resilient infrastructure.
  3. Align with Government Priorities & Investment: Morocco plans to triple the number of startups by 2030 and increase digital export revenues.
  4. Showcase Your Solutions on a Global Stage: Get involved in Morocco's digital transformation by connecting with industry leaders, policymakers, and local innovators.
  5. Leverage a Historic Moment: With the 2030 World Cup on the horizon, there is a need for advanced data management, urban intelligence, and strong digital infrastructure.
