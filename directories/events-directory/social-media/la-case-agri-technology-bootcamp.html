La Case Agri Technology Bootcamp

This four-day bootcamp, "Data & AI Bootcamp: Promoting smart agriculture in CÃ´te d'Ivoire," aims to train participants in using data and AI tools to develop innovative solutions for smart agriculture. The program, a collaboration between the Ivorian government (Ministry of Digital Transition and Digitalisation), GIZ, ESTDEV, and Expertise France, seeks to build capacity in the local startup ecosystem and reduce the digital divide in the agricultural sector.

The bootcamp will cover:

  • Day 1: Data Spaces & Governance, focusing on structuring, sharing, and monetizing agricultural data.
  • Day 2: Data Science & AI for Agriculture, teaching participants to extract insights and make predictions using data visualization, analysis, and machine learning.
  • Day 3: Satellite Data for Smart Agriculture, focusing on using Earth Observation and satellite data for agricultural decision-making.
  • Day 4: Application & Innovation: AI Use Cases in Agriculture, where participants will apply learned concepts to real-life use cases, ideate, prototype, and pitch AI-based solutions.

The bootcamp is scheduled from July 1st to 4th, with sessions from 9 AM to 4 PM daily, including coffee and lunch breaks.
