Water Knowledge Europe 2024

Water Knowledge Europe 2024 will occur from 10 to 12 December at the Bluepoint Venues in Brussels. Organised by Water Europe, the event is an important opportunity to foster networking, exchange of experiences and collaboration between key players in the water supply chain. During the event, experts and representatives from the European Union will present the Horizon Europe funding opportunities for the water sector and offer valuable insights on how to create and manage successful project consortia.

The event includes presentations of project ideas with targeted meetings to create connections between potential partners. Side activities will include ‘Water Projects Europe’, dedicated to innovative solutions for PFAS management and the implementation of circular water cycles, and meetings of the new Water Europe expert groups. Networking spaces and a dedicated dinner will complete the programme, fostering partnership-building and the development of innovative projects at European and international levels.
