Western Africa Digital Governance Forum

The United Nations University Operating Unit on Policy-Driven Electronic Governance (UNU-EGOV), in collaboration with the Division for Public Institutions and Digital Government of the United Nations Department of Economic and Social Affairs (UN DESA) and with support from GIZ, is organizing the 2025 edition of the West Africa Digital Governance Forum (WADGov Forum).

This important initiative brings together official representatives from all West African countries working in the field of digital governance. The Forum aims to foster an open dialogue among nations, share experiences, identify opportunities for collaboration, and strengthen regional and bilateral partnerships.

Over time, the WADGov Forum is expected to become a key platform for coordinating digital governance initiatives across the region and for amplifying West Africa’s voice in the global digital transformation agenda.

Jason Fox, Tech Community Manager & Evangelist at FIWARE Foundation, will join the session “Harnessing Data for Climate Resilience and Agricultural Innovation in West Africa” on November 3, from 14:30 to 16:00 (WAT). He’ll present FIWARE Data Spaces and AgriTech solutions, showcasing how open technologies can drive smarter, more sustainable agriculture across the region — followed by a panel discussion with other experts in the field.
