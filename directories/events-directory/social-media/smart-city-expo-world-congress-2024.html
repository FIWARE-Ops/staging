Smart City Expo World Congress 2024

We are excited to announce FIWARE's participation at SCEWC24 with a community booth.

SCEWC has been an important event for us for many years, and we remain dedicated to shaping the development of Smart Cities alongside our partners. Showcasing our community's contributions and strengths is of utmost importance to us.

As an official Event Partner, we are honored to be present in Pavilion 3, Stand B11, where FIWARE Community members, partners, and projects will be prominently displayed.

We also encourage you to attend the roundtable discussion featuring Clara Pezuela. As part of the Enabling Technologies track, she will contribute to the conversation on "Shaping the Digital Future Ethically". This session is scheduled to take place in a dedicated room on Tuesday, November 5th, from 11:20am to 12:05pm.
