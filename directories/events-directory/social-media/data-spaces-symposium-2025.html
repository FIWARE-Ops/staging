FIWARE Conference: Data Spaces Symposium 2025

The Data Spaces Support Centre (DSSC) and the Data Spaces Business Alliance (the Gaia-X association for data and cloud, the International Data Spaces Association, the FIWARE Foundation, and the Big Data Value Association) are excited to announce that the next edition of the Data Spaces Symposium will take place at the Hilton Hotel in Warsaw, from 11-12 March during the Polish ICT Week in 2025. After the incredible success of March 2024, which hosted over 900 participants, this gathering will highlight the value of data sharing in Europe and beyond, unlocking business opportunities, and boosting the impact and market adoption. The event will also highlight the major achievements of the Data Spaces Support Centre, including the latest Data Spaces Blueprint, the Data Spaces Radar, and the Data Spaces Toolbox. Mark your calendars for the world-leading event on data spaces!
