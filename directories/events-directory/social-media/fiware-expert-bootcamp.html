FIWARE Expert Bootcamp

This course is designed to provide attendees with advanced knowledge of FIWARE technology while also allowing them to take the official FIWARE Expert certification exam free of charge on the final day (Although it is not required to take it in order to participate on the course). This course has been designed by the FIWARE Foundation in collaboration with La Vega Innova.

The final session offers the opportunity to take the FIWARE Expert exam in person.
