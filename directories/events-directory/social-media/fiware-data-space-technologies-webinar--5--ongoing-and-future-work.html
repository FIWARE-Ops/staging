FIWARE Webinar: FIWARE Data Space Technologies Webinar #5: Ongoing and Future Work

Are you ready to deep dive into the world of data spaces and learn how FIWARE can improve your organization's data-sharing strategies? This six-part webinar series will cover everything you need to know about FIWARE Data Space Technologies. This series is organized by the FIWARE Technical Steering Committee, with support from FIWARE Foundation.

In this session the presenters will discuss ongoing and future work related to the FIWARE Data Space connector and associated technologies. FDSC is a data space connector deeply aligned with existing standards and technologies, and this session will discuss subjects such as integration with Gaia-X clearing houses and the DOME marketplace.
