UITP Summit

FIWARE will be present at the UITP Global Public Transport Summit 2025, taking place from June 15–18 in Hamburg, Germany – one of the world’s leading events for public transport innovation and future mobility solutions.

FIWARE will be exhibiting in Hall 2, Booth A2442, thanks to the generous support of our member BizzTech, who is kindly sharing their booth with us. Together, we are showcasing groundbreaking technologies that are shaping the next generation of public transport.

At the booth, you will discover:

  • NeMo.bil – a BMWK-funded project showcasing FIWARE’s digital ecosystem for autonomous, on-demand mobility in rural areas. The system uses small swarm vehicles for short distances that join into convoys led by a larger unit for longer trips. FIWARE powers the architecture behind this resource-efficient public transport solution, enabling seamless coordination between vehicles, operators, users, and data spaces.

  • HAL8122 – BizzTech’s powerful agentic AI system, transforming urban transit ecosystems by analyzing, optimizing, and orchestrating citywide mobility. HAL8122 supports the development of dynamic, user-centered networks — and is a cornerstone of BizzTech’s vision for a “Metaverse for Mobility.”

With immersive digital twins, cross-platform simulations, and real-time transit coordination, this showcase is a must-see for all forward-thinking mobility innovators.
