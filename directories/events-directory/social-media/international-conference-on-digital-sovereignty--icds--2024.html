FIWARE Conference: International Conference on Digital Sovereignty (ICDS) 2024

From November 28 to 30, 2024, in Oslo, Norway, the International Conference on Digital Sovereignty (ICDS) 2024 will bring together global leaders and innovators in Oslo to discuss a crucial topic: the role of digital sovereignty in safeguarding national and corporate infrastructures. We are proud to announce that Chandra Challagonda, CEO of the FIWARE Foundation, will deliver an inspiring talk titled “Digital Sovereignty: Data Provenance, Interoperability, and Open Standards” on Thursday, 28 November, from 10:10 to 10:40 AM. His presentation will delve into the fundamental pillars of a secure and independent digital ecosystem:

  • Data Provenance: ensuring transparency and trust through traceable data origins.
  • Interoperability: enabling seamless communication between systems for greater resilience.
  • Open Standards: fostering innovation and autonomy with open solutions. Don’t miss the opportunity to join this discussion on the future of digital technology. Learn more about the Agenda on the website.
