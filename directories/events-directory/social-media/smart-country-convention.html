FIWARE Conference: Smart Country Convention

The Smart Country Convention (in short: SCCON) is Germany’s largest congress for digitization in the public sector. As a driving force for the future of a modern public administration and smart cities, SCCON is a must for all key players who want to drive digital change. Over the years the Federal Ministry of the Interior has been a close partner of the event. FIWARE has a strong connection with SCCON as a regular partner; the community even had FIWARE Global Summit within event days in 2019.

National and international top speakers will provide a mixture of expert knowledge, some inspiration and new ideas on the digital transformation of the public sector. The curated Expo makes digital solutions and applications tangible for visitors – digitization to touch. In interactive workshops and seminars, participants get the chance to experience trend-setting topics and to receive digital know-how. Networking areas and Satellite Events offer numerous touch points to make valuable contact with relevant people.
