DACH Netzwerktreffen at ViennaUP

On June 5, 2024, we cordially invite you to the FIWARE DACH Network Meeting as part of the ViennaUP Startup Festival in Vienna. Immerse yourself in the dynamic world of startups and experience exciting presentations from outstanding speakers within the FIWARE network.

Here's a sneak peek of our agenda:

  • Michael Maier (City of Vienna, Innovation Lab Team) will discuss IoT @ City of Vienna.
  • Ignacio Garcia (DS4SSC) will present DS4SSC's open call for pilots.
  • Andrea Battaglia will share insights about FIWARE 2.0.
  • Prof. Michael Prange (FH Kiel) & Vera Böhner (FIWARE) will delve into sustainable mobility by land, rail, and water.
  • Johannes Klinglmayr from the Linz Center of Mechatronics will open perspectives on value creation through networking.
  • Lukas Künzel introduces smart solutions for smart cities: GIS and IoT joined by the SuperConnector

The DACH Meeting offers a unique opportunity for German-speaking FIWARE enthusiasts to connect and benefit from high-quality presentations. Learn more about FIWARE 2.0, discover opportunities to participate in current bi-national funding projects, and be inspired by current use cases.

ViennaUP sets the entire city of Vienna abuzz with startup vibes, offering a diverse program including pitch sessions, hackathons, seminars, and numerous networking opportunities. Last year, the city of Vienna welcomed 14,000 participants from over 96 countries, and this year's event promises to be another resounding success.

We look forward to welcoming you to the FIWARE DACH Network Meeting and diving together into the future of technology!
