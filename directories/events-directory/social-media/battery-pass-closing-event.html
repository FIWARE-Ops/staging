FIWARE Conference: Battery Pass Closing Event

On 25 February, the Battery Pass consortium will host its closing event in Brussels to celebrate three years of progress in advancing the implementation of the battery passport in Europe and beyond.
Attendees will have the opportunity to hear from renowned experts to gain actionable insights, build partnerships and explore strategies to turn digital battery passports into a transformative blueprint for transparent, competitive and circular value chains.
The speaker line-up includes representatives from the European Commission, Transport & Environment, RECHARGE, ACEA, BASF, BMW, FIWARE, Fraunhofer IPK, DIN, DKE, Circulor, Systemiq, Umicore, ZIV, acatech, and many others.

To attend the event online, please complete the registration form: https://thebatterypass.eu/resources/registration/
