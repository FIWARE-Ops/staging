TSDSI Tech Deep Dive (TTDD) Conference

TTDD 2025 will center on the theme: “Standardising 6G: Architecting Intelligent, Secure, and Sustainable Networks & Services.” As global research advances and standardization efforts accelerate, this edition will address the foundational elements of 6G standardization, helping to shape inclusive, resilient, and future-ready digital infrastructure.

From 15–17 July 2025, the conference will feature six fully virtual technical sessions covering:

  • Innovative network architecture possibilities for 6G.
  • The role of AI in shaping 6G networks Integration challenges and future directions for Non-Terrestrial Networks, including Satellites, HAPS, and UAVs, with terrestrial infrastructure.Security in a hyper-connected 6G environment.
  • Management of service delivery architectures.
  • The OFDM dilemma and exploration of next-generation waveforms for 6G.

On 18 July, the final day will be held in hybrid mode, beginning with a workshop on “New Network and Service Aspects of 6G Ecosystems.” The event will conclude with a valedictory session featuring:

  • Key takeaways from technical discussions.
  • The TSDSI Outstanding Technical Contributions (OTC) Awards for FY 2024–25.

Chandra Challagonda (CEO, FIWARE Foundation) will take part in Session S5: Management and Service Delivery Architectures on 17 July from 14:00-16:15 IST/10:00-12:15 CEST. During this session, he will discuss data interoperability and FIWARE’s global contributions to it as part of a special segmant focused on the role of data management and service delivery starting from 15:25 IST/11:25 CEST.
