Environment and Sustainability

A new future for the Mar Menor

The Mar Menor is more than just a lagoon; it represents what can be achieved when science, technology, and collaboration come together. This story demonstrates that addressing even the most complex environmental challenges with innovative and sustainable solutions is possible. Thanks to FIWARE and the shared vision of its partners, Mar Menor has become an example of environmental renewal and a model for the future of planetary conservation.
